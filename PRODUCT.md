# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients (startups, founders, small businesses) looking for a freelance web designer/developer who stands out. They arrive from a portfolio link, referral, or search, and need to quickly assess quality, style, and professionalism. They want to feel confident they're hiring someone with genuine craft, not a template reseller.

## Product Purpose

INFRARED TECHNOLOGIES is a one-person freelance studio's brand site. It exists to convert visitors into project enquiries by demonstrating exceptional design and development skill through the site itself. Success: the visitor hits "Start a project" feeling certain this studio can deliver something distinctive.

## Brand Personality

Bold, precise, cinematic. Technical, intense, premium. Minimal, dark, confident. The brand leads with atmosphere and craft. It's an infrared signature: invisible to most, unmistakable to those who see it.

## Anti-references

- Generic agency/portfolio templates (bland Webflow themes, stock photography, safe corporate layouts, rounded-everything SaaS cream)
- Over-designed dark mode tropes (neon gradients, glassmorphism, crypto/gaming aesthetics, gratuitous particle effects)
- Anything that looks mass-produced or AI-generated

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** The site's own craft is the portfolio. Every interaction proves capability.
2. **Atmosphere over decoration.** Mood comes from restraint, timing, and depth, not from piling on effects.
3. **Precision signals expertise.** Typography, spacing, and motion should feel engineered, not improvised.
4. **Earned complexity.** Complexity (3D, scroll choreography) only where it serves the narrative. Never for spectacle alone.
5. **Infrared confidence.** Dark, assured, unhurried. The brand doesn't shout; it radiates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Adequate contrast ratios for text on dark backgrounds. Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements. Prefers-reduced-motion respected throughout (static fallback for all animations). Screen reader landmarks and alt text for meaningful content.
